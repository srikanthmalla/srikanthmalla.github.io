I am currently working as Research Engineer at Kinetic Automation (Stealth Startup). Prior to that I worked as Research Engineer at Honda Research Institute (HRI), USA. I believe in intelligent robots that will coexist and help humans someday in near future. I am working to make that happen. I completed my Master's in Robotics Engineering from Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) in September 2018. I am interested in problems related to Computer Vision and Machine Learning. I am currently working on Multi-Object Tracking and Future Trajectory Prediction related problems for Self Driving Cars. I previously worked on 3D object Detection and 2D/3D Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM). I also worked with Prof Katerina Fragkiadaki (CMU) during 2017 Summer on 3D scene understanding (vision based ego-motion estimation). During my bachelors (2015-16) I have also worked with Sebastian Scherer (CMU) on Motion Planning for UAVs.
