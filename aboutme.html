I am a Robotics Graduate Student at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI). My research interest are Planning and Controls. Currently, I am working on Inverse Optimal Control (Imitation Learning), where Human teaches the robot how to do a task, from which the robot learns the task easily. The necessity of this research is knowledge is always transferable (it is more easy and fast to transfer knowledge) and the robots should adapt to that kind of human learning procedure. And also, for higher DOF control planning takes too much time, so as the reinforcement learning. Hence to make the robots to learn and plan faster this Imitation Learning can be very useful.
